**PARISCV** is a trace-driven profiler for RISC-V that identifies instruction-level extensions and
application-specific accelerators to improve the performance and efficiency of ML workloads. By
analyzing execution traces, it surfaces the hotspots and instruction patterns that are the best
candidates for custom hardware.

It was presented as a poster at the 3rd Workshop on Open-Source Computer Architecture Research
(OSCAR), ISCA'24 {% cite ashuthosh2024pariscv %}. A live project page is available at
[ashuthosh.de/pariscv-site](https://ashuthosh.de/pariscv-site/).
