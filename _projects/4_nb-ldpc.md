An investigation of robust **GF(2^m) NB-LDPC decoders** on FPGA, benchmarking throughput, power,
and area against GPU implementations. The work explored high-level design strategies for
achieving high-throughput and low-power non-binary LDPC decoding, carried out as part of the ECHO
Indo-Portugal project.

The design strategies were published in *IEEE Design & Test* {% cite subramaniyan2022nbldpc %}.

Highlights:

- Non-binary LDPC decoding over GF(2^m) on reconfigurable hardware.
- Throughput / power / area benchmarking against GPU baselines.
- High-level design strategies for energy-efficient decoders.
